On Booking — Triggered Actions

Three things that happen immediately after a booking is created in Ax

✉️
Action 1
Booking Confirmation Email
Booking confirmation email is sent to the student with course details, date, location and portal login instructions.
👤 Manual send
🧾
Action 2 — COD students only
Invoice Generated & Sent
Invoice is created and emailed to the student (or organisation). Applies to Cash on Delivery payment type only.
👤 Manual send
💬
Action 3 — All students
Immediate SMS to Student
Instant SMS fired the moment booking is created. Thanks the student, asks them to check email and log into their Learner Portal.
⚡ Automated workflow (W)
